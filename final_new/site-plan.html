Overview

Purpose

Picture Perfect Moments exists to help people capture, share, and cherish life’s most beautiful moments. We offer professional photography, flexible rental equipment, and clear package options, making it simple for anyone to enjoy high-quality photos and services.

Audience

Our site serves two main groups: photography lovers and clients looking for reliable photography services. This includes families, couples, business professionals, and hobbyists seeking rentals or photo packages.

Dynamic Elements (JavaScript)

  • Interactive Equipment Gallery： On the Equipment Rental page, users can filter equipment cards by type (camera, lens, etc.), and each card shows a price that highlights when hovered.
  • Contact Form Feedback： On the Contact Us page, submitting the form shows a live thank-you message with your name without reloading the page.
  • Theme Switcher (Demo)： On the site plan, a button lets you toggle light/dark theme for preview (see upper右 corner!).
